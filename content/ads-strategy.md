# 📈 MoonLIT Arc Ad Campaign Strategy (v1.0)

> **Project:** PRJ-001 (MoonLIT Arc Pipeline)
> **Date:** 2026-03-05
> **Target Niches:** MedSpas, Veterinary Clinics

---

## 1. Google Ads Strategy (Search)

### Campaign: [Search] Reputation Management - High Intent
- **Keywords:**
  - `reputation management for medspas`
  - `get more google reviews for dental clinic`
  - `review automation for veterinary clinics`
  - `reputation management software for small business`
- **Ad Copy 1 (MedSpa Focus):**
  - **Headline:** Own Your Reputation. 10x Your MedSpa Reviews.
  - **Description:** Stop losing patients to competitors with better reviews. Automated review requests & gating. One-time fee, lifelong ownership.
- **Ad Copy 2 (Vet Focus):**
  - **Headline:** Be the Top-Rated Vet in Your City.
  - **Description:** Turn every happy pet owner into a 5-star review. Automate your reputation today. No monthly fees.

---

## 2. Meta Ads Strategy (Social)

### Campaign: [Lead Gen] Review Engine - One-Time Ownership
- **Objective:** Lead Generation (Instant Forms)
- **Targeting (US):** 
  - **Interests:** Small business owners, Spa, Medical spa, Veterinary medicine.
  - **Age:** 28-55
- **Targeting (India):**
  - **Interests:** Entrepreneurship, Business owners, Dental clinics.
  - **Locations:** Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi.
- **Creative:**
  - **Image:** A clean dashboard showing a spike in 5-star reviews next to a competitor's profile.
  - **Copy:** "Still paying $300/mo for Birdeye or Podium? Stop the bleeding. Get our white-labeled Review Engine for a one-time ownership fee. Forever yours."
