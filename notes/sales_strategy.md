# Sales Strategy: Landing the First Client

To land our first client for **MoonLIT Arc**, we need to move from "searching" to "selling." We have the leads and the products; now we need the pitch.

## 1. The "Reputation Engine" SaaS Pitch
**Target:** Dental/Vet clinics with < 4.5 star ratings or < 50 reviews.
**The Offer:** "I'll help you get 20+ five-star reviews this month on autopilot, or you don't pay."

**The Script (SMS/WhatsApp):**
> "Hi [Name], saw [Business Name] on Maps. You guys have great service but the review count doesn't reflect it. I built a 'Review Engine' that automates follow-ups to happy customers. Can I send a 1-min demo of how it works for a clinic like yours?"

## 2. The "Automated Workflow" Pitch
**Target:** Any high-paying niche using manual booking or follow-ups.
**The Offer:** "I'll automate your lead follow-up so you never lose a booking again."

**The Script:**
> "Hi [Name], I noticed you don't have a website/booking bot. You're likely losing 30% of leads who call after hours. I build AI automations that text back missed calls instantly. Want to see a quick example?"

## 3. The "Free Value" Opener
**Strategy:** Give them a small win first.
- **Action:** Find a clinic with a broken link or bad info.
- **Message:** "Hey [Name], just a heads up—your Maps link is pointing to a dead page. I'm an automation dev and noticed it while doing research. Fixed it in a demo environment for you—want the link?"
